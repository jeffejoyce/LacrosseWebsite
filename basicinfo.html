This section of the website will focus on specific attributes of being a goalie. We will focus on stickwork, reaction time, athleticism, and other aspects of the position.

Stickwork
Goalies need to be able to pass and catch just like any other player on the field
During clears the goalies will need to be able to throw passes from five to fifty yards depending on the scenerio
Reaction Time
Goalies must have quick reaction time in order to make saves
Goalies must have good hand eye coordination so they can save the ball using their stick
Reaction time can be developed using various hand eye coordination drills
Athleticism
Goalies need to have good footwork in order to move properly when making a save
Goalies need to be in fit condition so they can accomplish all of the tasks expected of the goaltender
Leadership
Goalies need to have a leadership quality as they will need to control the defense
Goalies organize the defense that the team runs and when they run them
The goalie is the ingame defense leader while the game is in play
Craziness
All goalies have a little bit of crazy in there system. Anyone who is willing to stand in front of a rubber ball being shot between 70-90mph has to be.
This is a common joke amoungst players and coaches about playing Goalie
