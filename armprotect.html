Arm Protection

Elbow Pads

Elbow pads are only used in Mens lacrosse. Elbow pads are used to protect the elbow from stick checks from the opposing team. They come in a variety of lengths and different lengths provide a variety of padding. The players selects what type of elbow pad they want to wear. Normally offensive players wear larger elbow pads and defensive players wear smaller elbow pads.

Gloves

Gloves are worn in both Mens and Womens Lacrosse. Womens lacrosse uses gloves with little to no padding. In the Womens game gloves are mostly used to create better grip on the stick. Gloves are not required in the Womens lacrosse. In Mens Lacrosse gloves provide protection from stick checking from the opposing team. Gloves are required in Mens lacrosse. Gloves have a padded exterior but the palm of the glove is a mess that allow for a better grip of the stick. Also goalies wear special gloves that provide better protection of the thumb and fingers. Gloves also have cuff link pads to protect the wrist.
