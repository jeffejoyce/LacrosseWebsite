Chest Protection

Shoulder Pads

Shoulder pads are used in Mens lacrosse to protect players from checks to the chest, shoulder, and upper back region. Shoulder pads come in a variety of shapes and sizes depending on the player. Shoulder pads are required in Mens lacrosse.

Rib Pads

Rib pads are a pad used in the Mens game only. They are worn below the shoulder pads to protect the ribs. These pads may be optional depending on the level of play.

Chest Protector

Chest protectors are worn by goalies in both Mens and Womens lacrosse. The chest protector is a pad that protects goalies from the shoulders down to the waist. The chest protector has a solid piece of plastic in the center of the chest to to protect the sternum. There is no protection on the back of the chest protector.
