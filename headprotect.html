Head Protection

Helmets

Helmets are worn in both Mens and Womens Lacrosse. Every player in Mens lacrosse is required to wear a helmet. The helmets come in a variety of manufactures but all helmets have a plastic shell with a titanium bar face mask. The face mask allows for a wide view angle for the player while still properly protecting them. In Womens lacrosse helmets are only worn by the goalies. This allows for the goalies to be properly protected from the opposing team shots.

Goggles

Goggles are worn in the Womens game only. They are worn by every player on the field except for the goalie. The goggles cover only the eyes. They are similar to glass but made of titanium bars to all for better viewing angles. The goggles have head bands that securely tightly to the head so the goggles do not slide out of place.
