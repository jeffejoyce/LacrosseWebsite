Womens Lacrosse

Womens Lacrosse has a lot of similarities to the men's game. The general difference between Womens Lacrosse and Mens is the Mens game can be more physical. Women's lacrosse still has some physical aspects but there is not body checking in Womens lacrosse. The equipment in Womens lacrosse is goggles and a mouthguard. If you need more information on the equipment please see the Equipment section of the website Below are the general rules of Womens Lacrosse.

1. Number of players on the field: 12
2. Number of players on offense: 7
3. Number of players on defense: 7
4. Womens lacrosse has draws while Mens lacrosse has face offs
5. Womens lacrosse sticks have a flat pocket

Men's Lacrosse

Mens lacrosse is a very physical game compared to Womens lacrosse. Mens lacrosse requires much more equipment to be worn due to the physical nature. Mens lacrosse has two different styles of sticks depending on whether you play offense or defense.

1. Number of players on the field: 10
2. Number of players on offense: 6
3. Number of players on defense: 6
4. Mens lacrosse has three player assigned to offense and defense. Then three players who play both offense and defense
5. Mens lacrosse allows for defense players to have sticks of a longer length. This allows for more agressive checking and more difficult stick work.
